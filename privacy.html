Privacy Policy

Vintage Social Counter collects and stores only the information needed to connect supported social accounts, display approved metrics, and keep published board links working.

Data We Collect

When you connect a social account, we may store tokens, account identifiers, profile display information, and metric values such as followers, likes, and views.

How We Use Data

We use this data to authenticate with platform APIs, refresh metrics, keep your connection active, and render your private dashboard and public board links.

Public Display

If you publish a board, the selected metrics shown on that board are visible to anyone with the link. Private connection credentials are not displayed on the public board.

Storage and Security

Connection data is stored on the server so the app can continue working across restarts. Sensitive tokens should be protected using server-side security controls and encryption where available.

Third-Party Services

This service relies on third-party APIs such as YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok. Those platforms may also process data according to their own privacy policies and developer terms.

Your Choices

You may disconnect a linked account at any time. You may also stop publishing a public board by removing or changing the board from the creator dashboard.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact the app owner using the support information provided with the service.
